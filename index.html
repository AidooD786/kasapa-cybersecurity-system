About This Project

This cybersecurity management system was developed as part of a field trip assessment of Kasapa FM's IT and OT infrastructure at Ghana Communication Technology University.

The system addresses identified vulnerabilities including weak access control, ransomware exposure, broadcast signal hijacking risks, and insufficient backup procedures.

Implementation follows the NIST Cybersecurity Framework with agile methodology across four sprints:

  • Asset identification & risk assessment
  • Access control deployment
  • Network segmentation & firewall installation
  • Staff training & system testing
